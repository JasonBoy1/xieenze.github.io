Enze Xie (谢恩泽)

CV / GitHub / Google Scholar / Email: Johnny_ez AT 163 DOT com | xieenze AT hku dot hk

I am currently a first year PhD student in The University of Hong Kong (HKU), supervised by Dr. Ping Luo. I am also a research intern at Sensetime, a Chinese start-up company of computer vision. Before that, I was a research intern at Face++ Detection Group, a Chinese start-up company of computer vision, supervised by Dr. Gang Yu. I also work close with Dr. Chunhua Shen. I did several works on text detection in Face++, now I am interested in object detection and instance segmentation.

I obtained my bachelor degree from Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics (南京航空航天大学) and master degree from TongJi University (同济大学).
